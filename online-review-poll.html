Online Reviews - Poll

How much trust do your customers place in reviews about your business?

Online Reviews are big business. Some companies even go to the trouble of faking 5* reviews just to get your attention. Other companies clearly don't give a stuff about what people say about them whilst the majority are conscientious and respond to them with outward concern and diligence.

But what's the real story? What do you think when you see an online review that's 5 Stars? Too good to be true, or a reflection of a great company/product? If you see 3.5 Stars, do you think it's a refelction that you can't please everyone all of the time, or that the product/company has some flaws/shortcomings that need addressing.

In this short, un-scientific poll, we're asking just one simple question ... "Which average rating gives you most confidence when you're looking to buy or make an enquiry?"
